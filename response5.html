Hello,

I honestly didn’t know that CD PROJEKT RED was so blatantly pro-Nvidia. It’s disappointing because I thought this kind of favoritism was more of a community issue. However, after the latest update, it’s clear that this is not the case.

It’s hard to understand how, with FSR 3.1 available on AMD since June, they haven’t bothered to implement it, even after several updates. It’s a great game, but all users should be treated equally, regardless of their platform or GPU. It’s unacceptable that I always have to rely on mods to fix compatibility issues simply because I don’t use Nvidia. That shouldn’t be the case.

And what about console players who don’t even have that option? The excuse of performance or technical limitations doesn’t hold up. I have an RX 7900 XTX, which is more than capable of running the game on ultra settings. But due to the lack of compatibility with AMD, it will never perform as well as a lower-tier Nvidia card. It’s frustrating to have a 24 GB VRAM graphics card and still need to use workarounds to enjoy the game properly.

You should listen more to the community. We are the ones who support your projects and, even if we’re not always right, we often provide constructive feedback. As players, we spend countless hours on your games, we know their weaknesses, and we see where things could be improved. Taking this kind of feedback into account is essential to continue creating high-quality products.

Also, while you’ve significantly improved the game’s initial state (which was the bare minimum), you still haven’t delivered all the promised content. The community hasn’t forgotten that.

In short, you have an amazing product, but it’s disheartening to see part of your audience being ignored. I hope this message isn’t misinterpreted; my intention is to provide constructive criticism.

Thank you for reading, and best regards.
