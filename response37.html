These are mostly observations on PS4 1.5 (Slim with internal SSD), though there are some bugs which I will highlight in bold. Over previous update versions I replayed the game around a dozen times, so feel I know the game's quirks pretty well.

Firstly, intro movie runs choppy, worse than before - very badly. Don't understand why, as it's a pre-rendered video file.

V's footstep sounds really exagerrated, as though they are wearing diving boots. Klomp klomp klomp.

Game looks as though it has received a visual downgrade? It looks worse than before. Textures are blotchy, in photo mode I observed them moving - like resizing? in real time (weird, will try to capture this) very low-res, weird particle effects, shadows, reflective surfaces - as though screen space reflections have gone crazy. Looks like a moving watercolour painting sometimes.

Not seeing an improvement in performance either, in case that was the indended tradeoff, overall seems worse.

Dialogue subtitles persist after lines are spoken. Example: Jackie's "Shoot V, shoot!" lines from the Scav chase stayed there well after I arrived at V's Megabuilding.

Stealth feels broken, enemies are seeing me and unconscious bodies through walls, floors even when their backs are turned.

Car engine sounds very quiet.

Perk points resetting after saving my game and reloading later.

Could not scan Yorinobu's datapad during Evelyn's BD until I had quit/reloaded.
