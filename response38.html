Dear developers
I started a new playtrough on very hard and discovered very hard is still doable and there are a lot of positive changes made. Driving is improved (suspension etc.), water changes, skipping time animation timer changes a nice touch, same with skill progression bars, the small graphic details added, it makes a lot of difference, the game looks much more polished overall. The Heist roof top guards change make me smile. And is it me or new sounds were added?
That being said I would like to touch on the negatives, the things making the playing experience less fun.
1) The game was purposefully made harder, in 1.5 (in order to last longer I guess), and that would be OK IF the nerfing had been done as a toggle option and a community been informed in advance. Apply nerfing weapons to hard and very hard is OK but as a blanket nerfing it doesn’t make sense, is not a multiplayer game, so you just screwed players ongoing builds. Bullet sponge comes to mind. Again.
2) Most aggravating for me was arcade style shoot the drones with a pistol, at the end of the Heist. You are not allowed even to exchange the weapon with another one from inventory. Why??? The difficulty was raised making it terribly difficult even downgrading the difficulty to EASY. Make it shippable. It does not add to the story or enjoyment, it just add aggravation. I don’t know how other vehicle shooting were modified after that as I was not able to progress further in 1.5. Big let down. Making it difficult for the sake of it does not improve the game. Especially hard when computer is dropping frames, at 10-20 fps it becomes a nightmare to drive or shoot. You should try (at 20fps) that chase yourselves, and not allowed to have a toilet break until you beat it
3) Quick navigation is disabled for me, for some reason, clicking on blue dots does nothing so now I have to drive all over the map. Anyone else experiencing this with or without mods? The good side is that I get to experience more great visuals moving through Night City at a slower pace.
4)Is skipping main quest an option? For the people who already finished the game, could you add at least an optional skip sequence for all, and I mean ALL cinematic sequences and dialogues we are railroaded through, including Johnny Silverhand intermezzo, the few mandatory car chase, Panam tank shooting tutorial, scavs and last mission tank shooting , power plant drone chase, UAV drone chase, and BD scanning for River, for Peralez, the Flathead, Johnny helicopter scene and capture, V encounter with Johnny in the apartment, Victor’s first implant scene, Victor’s scene start act 2, and before last mission etc., let us save anywhere, and yes, that includes the Secret Ending, and so on, you get the picture. Yes, we love the game, that’s why some of us played through multiple times, we did that, we’ve seen those scenes, many many times, and for my part I would like to have the option to skip them now, with the click of a button. And just enjoy Night City.
I am grateful you made at least the BD tutorial in Judy’s basement skippable. That’s a start.
5) For some reason there is to much sonic pollution in certain areas. I am talking about multiple radio sources blasting the same song. Sometimes an annoying feedback effect happens like when you have a speaker close to a microphone kind of thing. I don’t know why you have to have so many sound sources crowded in a small area?
That being said, soundtrack overall is so good, makes me want to buy a CD. Or few of them for that matter. I noticed in the last playtrough, a very nice drone like sound track while moving to the city. Much closer to the Blade Runner-Technomancer atmospheric sound that Night City should have. Congrats to the audio team.
